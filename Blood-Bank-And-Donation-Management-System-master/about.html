About this system

This demo app helps manage blood donors locally in your browser. It is built as a simple, professional front-end to demonstrate donor registration, searching, requesting compatible donors, and CSV import/export.

For guidance on donating, see the Donate Info guides (eligibility, preparing, after donation care).

Why donate?

  • Save lives — a single donation can save multiple patients.
  • Healthy donors give regularly and help maintain safe blood supply.
  • Every donation is tested and processed for safety.

How to use

  1. Use "Add Donor" to register donors.
  2. Use "Donors" to search, edit or delete entries.
  3. Use "Request Blood" to find compatible donors by blood group and city.
