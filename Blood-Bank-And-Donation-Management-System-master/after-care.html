After donation care

After donating, follow basic care steps to recover quickly and safely.

Immediately after donation

  • Stay seated or lying down for a few minutes. Drink fluids and have a snack provided by the center.
  • Keep the bandage on for at least a few hours; avoid vigorous activity with the arm.

24–48 hours after

  • Drink more fluids and eat iron-rich foods to help replenish blood.
  • Avoid heavy lifting or strenuous exercise for the remainder of the day.
  • If you experience prolonged dizziness, bleeding, or severe pain at the site, contact the blood center or seek medical help.

These are general tips — follow the advice of local health professionals.
