Preparing to donate blood

Small steps before donation help ensure a safe and comfortable experience.

Before you go

  • Eat a healthy meal a few hours before donating and avoid fatty foods.
  • Drink plenty of water the day before and on the day of donation — hydration helps maintain blood pressure.
  • Get a good night's sleep to feel rested.
  • Bring a valid ID and any relevant medical information.

On arrival

  • Inform staff about recent illnesses, medications, travel, or tattoos.
  • Expect a short physical check (blood pressure, hemoglobin).
  • If you feel anxious, let staff know — they can help with comfort measures.

Follow local blood center instructions for full details.
